frogs start out as eggs

as the tadpoles mature they start to grow legs

Everyone loves inspirational quotes about butterflies. You know... the caterpilliar transforming and flying to a better place. But have you ever concidered that maybe life is more like being a frog? That that expodential growth doesn't happen in the saftey of cacoon, but rather growth happens as life happens, and as time goes on, you go from swimming, to developing little legs, until finally you can hop out of the water onto land to explore the wider world. Only to eventually be eaten by a bird, or a cat. The end.
